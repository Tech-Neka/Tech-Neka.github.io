CLOCKS AND TIMERS

Time keeping is as old as time itself. Indeed from the ancient hour-glass to the millenial digital stop watches, humans have always found it necessary to keep track of their daily activities with regards to time. And today, clocks and timers come in every possible shape and size in every industry where they are required, as very useful gadgets.

DIGITAL CLOCKS: Popular industries that utilise digital clocks include the Travel and Tourism industry with digital clock installed among flight and travel instruments.

TIMERS: Food processing gadgets such as microwave ovens come woth pre-installed timers to ensure adequate timing in food preparation.
