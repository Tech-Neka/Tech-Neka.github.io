APTITUDE QUIZ OVERVIEW


Welcome to the Aptitude Quiz. This quiz is to test your basic Numeracy and Literacy skills.
There are 20 questions in all. Ten for Numeracy and ten for Literacy.

Most questions are multiple choice and require you to pick an answer among options.
Others require you to input your calculated answer in the given input-spaces.

For the Numeracy Quiz, only include numbers or decimal points in your answers.
Any other answer form will not be accepted by the computer.

The timer is set at 20 minutes to test your speed giving 1 minute to each question.
However, you can still continue the quiz when it's done.



To begin the Quiz, click the 'START' button on the next page.

And Click the 'SUBMIT' button to view your score when completed.

Good Luck!!
