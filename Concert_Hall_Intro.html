OVERVIEW


We have all been part of an audience at one time or another, be it at a lecture auditorium, a movie hall or even a church sermon, where we must be seated on a flat ground most likely behind someone else. We all know the frustration of not being able to see the front where all the activity is going on.

This app was designed in response to consumers requests and necessity to choose or select their sitting positions while booking for a desired event. It takes into consideration the heights of the participants or the height of the seats given. Seats are allocated on a first-come-first-served basis, therefore viewers can only choose from available seats and must choose wisely based on given inputs.

Plese click the "Next" button below and play the game of numbers to find which seats will be most suitable for viewing the concert stage!!

In this game, the choice is yours!!
