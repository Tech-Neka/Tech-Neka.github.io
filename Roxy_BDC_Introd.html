BUSINESS PROJECT DESCRIPTION

INTRODUCTION

The financial sector and lending markets in West Africa is greatly booming and there is no shortage of vendors, merchants or customers. This industry include banks, insurance brokers, bureaus de change and other financial investors. The Central Bank which is the apex bank, governs the financial brokers. It standarizes and regulates the activities and sets merchant-profit rates to the immense benefit of consumers. Bureaus de Change are unique in their own right and provide primarily foreign exchange to customers of all professions for various purposes

ROXY BDC

Roxy BDC is an upcoming FOREX trading Bureau de Change in the suburban area of a West African city, which seeks to increase business profits. It's an SME business that has its gaze on growth and expansion. The business owner and CEO wants to understand his business's outflows and maximise on its profitability. Subsequently, it has requested an overview of it's activities by an analyst. It's business request letter outlines the details of it's main areas of analysis and business bottlenecks.


BUSINESS QUESTIONS

  1. Who are the most likely people to buy FOREX?
  2. When are the peak sales periods including hidden trends?
  3. What is the average weekly sale per customer?
  4. What is the average yearly buy in dollars?
  5. Are sales increasing from one year to the next?
  6. Why are sales increasing from one year to the next?
  7. Are we getting new customers?
  8. How did most customers hear about us?
  9. Is our marketing strategy good?
  10. What percentage of customers are foreigners?
  11. How can we increase customer base?
